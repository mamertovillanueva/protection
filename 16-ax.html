Life of Jesus and Mary

Ax to the Foot

I now come against all patterns of endless sufferings, including the need to suffer and the need to fsil. I break all the pathways of suffering from feeling useless, worthless, and hopeless. I come against all repeated patterns of broken hearts, feelings of rootlessness, and not belonging. I come all paralysis. I say, “NO” to all patrons of rejection, bitterness, resentment, and unforgiveness. I renounce all roadways to evil, negativity, and unloved in my ancestry, in the Name of Jesus Christ.

Lord, I ask You now to bring to mind any ingrained patterns that You are ready to heal, any pattern of shame, pain, or grief. Reveal the specific ancestral patterns that You now are saying, “Now is the time of deliverance. “ Lord, I ask You now in a sovereign sweep of Your Spirit to send forgiveness
