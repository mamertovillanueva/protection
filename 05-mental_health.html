Mental Health

With the power of the Blood of Jesus, I now break all patterns of mental illness and insanity coded into my ancestral system. I come against all abnormal anti-social behavior, paranoia, schizophrenia, passive-aggressive patterns, personal disorders, and nervous habits. I come against all inflexibility, perfectionism, manic-depressive patterns, and strange ways. I stop all wounding and repressing masculinity. I bring an end to generational patterns of repressing and damaging the female spirit.

I seal off the hidden pathways of self-destruction that have tunneled through my family history. Lord, saturate these areas with your forgiveness and peace.

Father, let mental health and wholeness be written into the fabric of my bloodline. Let each one have the most of Christ. Let clear thinking, emotional balance, and healthy patterns of relating come forth. I come against deep dark patterns of emotional and spiritual heaviness -inability to play, have fun, and express joy.

I ask you, Jesus, for a spirit of laughter and lightness to rise to my bloodline. Thank you. Lord Jesus.
