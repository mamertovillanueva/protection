Life of Jesus and Mary

Joyful Mysteries.

(meditate every sat. and mon.)

The first joyful mystery is the Annunciation of Gabriel to Mary.

The second joyful mystery is the Visitation of Mary to Elizabeth.

The third joyful mystery is The Birth of Our Lord.

The fourth joyful mystery is The Presentation of Our Lord.

The fifth joyful mystery is The Finding of Our Lord in the Temple.


Sorrowful Mysteries

(meditate every tue. and fri.)

The first mystery is The Agony of Our Lord in the Garden

The second mystery is Our Lord is Scourged at the Pillar

The third mystery is Our Lord is Crowned with Thorns

The fourth mystery is Our Lord Carries the Cross to Calvary

The fifth mystery is The Crucifixion of Our Lord


Glorious Mysteries

(meditate every wed. and sun.)

The first glorious mystery is The Glorious Resurrection of Our Lord.

The second glorious mystery is The Ascension of Our Lord.

The third glorious mystery is The Descent of the Holy Spirit at Pentecost.

The fourth glorious mystery is The Assumption of Mary into Heaven.

The fifth glorious mystery is The Coronation of Mary as Queen of Heaven and Earth.


Luminous Mysteries

(meditate every thu.)

First luminous mystery is The Baptism of Our Lord in the River Jordan

The second mystery is The Wedding at Cana, when Christ manifested Himself

The third mystery is The Proclamation of the Kingdom of God

The fourth mystery is The Transfiguration of Our Lord

The fifth mystery is The Last Supper, when Our Lord gave us the Holy Eucharist
