Prayer for Protection

Eternal Father, we offer the Most Precious Blood of thy divine Son Jesus, in union with the masses, said throughout the world today, for the Holy Soul in purgatory, for sinners to the universal church, to their home, and within our families. Amen.

We place ourselves in the presence of Jesus Christ and submit to His Lordship. We "put on God's armor to be able to resist the evil's tactics". (Eph.6.10:11) We stand our ground, "with truth buckle around our waist and integrity for a breastplate." (Eph. 6.14)

We carry the "shield of faith" to put out the burning arrow of the evil one" (Eph. 6.16)

We accept "salvation from God to be our helmet and to receive the Spirit is to use as a sword." (Eph. 6.17)

In the name of Jesus Christ, crucified, died, and risen. We bind all the spirits of the air, the atmosphere, the water, the fire, the wind, the ground, the underground, and the netherworld.

We also bind the influence of any lost or fallen souls who may be present, and all the emissaries of the satanic headquarters or any coven of witches or warlocks, or satan worshippers who may be present in a preternatural way.

We claim the blood of Jesus in the air and atmosphere, the water, the fire, the wind, the ground, and their fruits all around us, the underground and the netherworld.

In the Name of Jesus Christ, we forbid every adversary mentioned to communicate with or help one another in any way to communicate with us or to do all except what We command in Jesus' Name.

In the Name of Jesus Christ, we seal this place, and all presents, and all family and associates of those present, and their places and possessions and sources of supply in the Blood of Jesus. (3x)

In the Name of Jesus Christ by the merit of His Precious Blood, We dissolve every curse, hex, seal, spell, sorcery, bond, snare, trap, device, lie, stumbling block, obstacle, deception, diversion or distraction, spiritual chain or spiritual influence, also every disease of body, soul, mind or spirit placed upon us or on this place, or any of the persons, places, things mentioned, by any agent, or brought on us by our own mistakes or sins. (3x)

We now place the cross of Jesus Christ between myself and all generations in our family tree. We say in the Name of Jesus Christ that there will be no direct communication between the generations. All communications will be filtered through the Precious Blood of the Lord Jesus Christ.

Mary the Immaculate, clothe us in the light, power, and energy of your faith. Father, please assign the angels and saints to assist us.

Thank you, Lord Jesus, for being our wisdom, our justice, our sanctification, our redemption.

We surrender to the ministry of Your Holy Spirit, and receive Your truth concerning intergenerational healing.

Glory be to the Father, and to the Son, and to the Holy Spirit.

As it was in the beginning, is now and ever shall be world without end. Amen.
