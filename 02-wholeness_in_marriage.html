Wholeness in Marriage

In the Name of Jesus Christ, I come against all deeply ingrained patterns of marital unhappiness in my bloodline. I say “No” to all suppression of my spouse and to all expressions of marital love.

I stop all hatred, death wishes, evil desires, and intentions in a marriage relationship. I put an end to all transmission of violence, all vindicated negative behavior, all infidelity and deception. I halt coded transmission that blocked enduring relationships.

I renounce all patterns of family tension, divorce, and hard hearts. In the name of Jesus, I bring an order end to all deeply ingrained patterns of feeling trapped in an unhappy marriage and feeling of emptiness and failure.

Father, forgive my relatives for all the ways they have dishonored the Sacrament of Marriage. Please bring forth in my bloodline many deeply committed marriages, love, faithfulness, fidelity, and kindness.
